Our Journey Since 2001

We have been in the iron & steel industry since 2001 under the name of Raj Steel Agro Industry. A thousand mile journey starts with one single step. Our quality brought laurels and goodwill instantly and the rest is history.

Strong values of a family-run enterprise backed by industry's most talented professionals, along with the entry of the third generation, led to the foundation of Raj Agro Industries in 2010.

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality Spring Steel Flats with a state-of-the-art facility spread over 13,500 sq. yards in Mandi Gobindgarh (Punjab), North India. We're one of the first plants in the region operating on PNG gas, with the industry's best production and technical staff having over a decade of experience.

Our quality and fair business practices have earned us reputation and appreciation from clients in core industries like Automobile and Railways.

Manufacturing Facilities

Spring steel refers to high carbon or alloy steel used in the manufacture of springs. The steel is heat-treated to develop specific properties, making it ideal for applications requiring the ability to return to its original shape. Our Spring steel flats are produced using advanced technology for precisely controlled thickness.
